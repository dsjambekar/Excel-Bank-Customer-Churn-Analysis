# Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn patterns in a retail bank to identify key drivers of attrition and quantify revenue risk.

## Business Problem
Customer churn leads to significant revenue loss. The goal is to identify high-risk customer segments and actionable drivers of churn.

## KPIs
- Overall Churn Rate
- Revenue at Risk
- Complaint Churn Rate
- High-Value Customer Churn Rate
